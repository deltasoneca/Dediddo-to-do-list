Serious security and performance
for your critical Excel spreadsheet.

SpreadServe takes your calculation heavy spreadsheet into a robust cloud server for automated operation on a shared, secure, logged and audited environment.

Your Excel spreadsheet is your professional tool, it deserves a professional server.

Features

Complete Excel compatibility

SpreadServe supports .xls and .xlsx formats, XLL addins, RTD & VBA.
You won't change a thing on how your work!
Develop your spreadsheets in Excel, then operate them in SpreadServe.

Data Flow Automation

SpreadServe has ready-made integrations including RDBMS connectors and REST API.
Data can flow automatically to and from databases, message buses, and websites.
Increase how much of your work can be automated.

Scalability and Performance

Don't worry about how massive your spreadsheets are or how many people use them.
SpreadServe can operate as many spreadsheet engines as you need to handle your workload.

Resilient Environment

SpreadServe has server class job control, monitoring, file logging and DB logging for robust operations.
No more lost work or data!
Your workload professionally handled.

Security by design

There's one deployment of your critical spreadsheet, and access is authenticated.
No more emailed copies of critical spreadsheets everywhere, all in different versions.
Maintain control and keep your data safe.

Testable and Auditable

SpreadServe logs all your critical spreadsheet operations in a database.
And runs script unit tests for your spreadsheets in Python for automated regression testing.
Remove the fragility of your spreadsheet.

Give Excel superpowers.
